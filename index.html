Crystals Collector

A Random Number in Red generates in the middle.

There are Four Crystals below. Each crystal contains a Hidden Number Value.

By clicking on one of the Crystals you will add that Value to your Current Number in Blue.

You win the game by matching your Current Number to the Random Number.

You lose the game if your Current Number goes above the Random Number.

Upon winning or losing, the game will change the Values of each Crystal.
